About Conference

Data Analytics is the science of analysing raw data to make conclusions about that information. The techniques and processes of data analytics have been automated into mechanical processes and algorithms that work over raw data for human consumption. Data analytics help a business optimize its performance. The Second International Conference on Data Analytics & Learning-2022 (DAL'22), organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering, Alva’s Institute of Engineering & Technology, Moodbidri, Mangalore, Karnataka, India in association with the DEPARTMENT OF STUDIES IN COMPUTER SCIENCE, UNIVERSITY OF MYSORE, MYSURU, KARNATAKA, INDIA. The International Conference on Data Analytics & Learning(IC DAL’22) invites the original manuscripts from the scholars of industry & academia. The conference comprises Keynote Address in the field of Data Analytics & Learning by eminent International speakers both from Industry & Academia. The conference will be held in hybrid mode.


About AIET

Alva’s Institute of Engineering & Technology (AIET) is a premier Engineering Institute of Alva’s Education Foundation established in the year 2008. The institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, and approved by AICTE. There are a total of eight UG programs namely Computer Science and Engineering, Information Science and Engineering, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Computer Science and Design,Electronics and Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering and Agricultural Engineering. Also AIET has a PG program in Master of Business applications. The department of Computer Science and Engineering and Electronics and Communication Engineering are accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. AIET is 18 KMs from Mangalore International Airport and is strategically located adjacent to Mangalore Solapur National Highway-169. It has a lush green campus spread over 30 acres; a part of ALVAS-SHOBHAVANA, 144 acres spread on a famous herbal garden with more than two thousand varieties of herbal plants.
